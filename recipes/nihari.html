Nihari (Spicy beef stew)

Description

Spicy meat stew. Slow cooked till tender with thick gravy.

Great hearty meal for cold winters with a roll of bread

Dry Ingredients

  • 1 tbls Kashmiri Chilli or Smoked Paprika
  • 1 tbls Red Chilli
  • 1 tbls Salt
  • 1 tps Cumin Powder
  • 1 tps Ginger Powder
  • 1 tps Turmericr
  • 1 tps Citric Acid

Cooking steps

  1. Fry in a large pot 2 finely chopped onions till gold brown
  2. Add 2 Tbls ginger garlic paste fry until gold brown
  3. Add the meat, beef or mutton
  4. Add all dry ingredients
  5. Saute meat with spices for 10 minutes until braised
  6. Add 2 cups broth or water
  7. Boil for 10 minutes
  8. Slow cook for 2 hours
